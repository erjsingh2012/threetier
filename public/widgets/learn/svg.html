SVG Words & Letters — Interactive Playground

Tip: type a word and choose a mode. The SVG uses a web-safe font fallback and basic effects.

How it works

This playground demonstrates several ways to create words and letters with SVG:
  • <text> for simple text and styling
  • stroke with stroke-dasharray & stroke-dashoffset to animate outlines
  • <textPath> to map text onto a curve
  • splitting letters into individual <tspan> elements for per-letter transforms/animations
